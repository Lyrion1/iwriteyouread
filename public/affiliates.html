We're seeking thoughtful partnerships with podcasters, content creators, and media platforms whose audiences would appreciate deep, nuanced conversations about politics, philosophy, and culture.

For Podcast Hosts

Are you hosting conversations that matter? Shows like I've Had It, The Breakfast Club, or similar platforms that dive deep into politics, culture, and contemporary issues are perfect fits for collaboration.
