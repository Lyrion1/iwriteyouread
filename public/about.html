About Alexander Afolabi

A Voice for Thoughtful Discourse

Alexander Afolabi is an independent writer, author, and political commentator whose work explores the intersections of democracy, culture, and identity. With a sharp analytical mind and a commitment to intellectual honesty, Alexander challenges conventional narratives and invites readers to engage with complex ideas about society and governance.

His writing spans books, essays, and commentary on issues ranging from immigration policy and political philosophy to cultural criticism and social justice. Through iwriteyouread, Alexander creates a space for thoughtful dialogue that transcends partisan divisions.

Author of The Spirit of America: Views from the Other Side, Alexander brings a unique perspective to American political discourse, examining the nation's foundational values and contemporary challenges through the eyes of an independent thinker unafraid to question both left and right.
